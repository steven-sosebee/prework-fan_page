Frank Sinatra

Bio

This crooner got his first big break when he was paired up with the Three Flashes for the Major Bowes and His Original Amateur Hour . In July 1939, Frank recorded his first record, From The Bottom of My Heart. (1)

His movie career took off with Anchors Aweigh and continued over the next few decades. (2)

Known as a member of the Rat Pack along with Sammy Davis Jr. and Dean Martin, the group continued to record music and star in films together.

Top Songs:

  • My Way
  • That's Life
  • New York, New York
